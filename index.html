Alvin Lee

Singer, songwriter, and Blues Rock legend

Here's a timeline of Alvin Lee's life

  • He was born Graham Anthony Barnes in Nottingham, UK, on 19 December 1944
  • He formed the band Ten Years After with bassist Leo Lyons
  • In 1969 Ten Years After gained wide exposure due to their appearance at the Woodstock Festival
  • In 1973 he decided to leave the band due to its limited style and the record company's preference towards pop music
  • He recorded numerous other albums in collaboration with stars such as Steve Winwood, George Harrison, and Jerry Lee Lewis
  • He died in 2013 in Spain during a routine surgery to correct an atrial arrhythmia
